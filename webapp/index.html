What We Do

RESAAAS is a cloud-based social business platform for the real estate services industry. We help you sell homes, earn referral business, explore your market, and make connections ... All in real time.

  • Agents

    Market your listings to a global audience and generate more business. Network with other agents to demonstrate your expertise of the industry and the markets you do business in.

    Learn More

  • Brokers

    Help your agents sell more, faster with our educational resource center. Get an overview of your brokerage and recruit top agents in your local market with the transparency of a global real estate network.

    Learn More

  • Franchises

    Improve communication within your organization by connecting your agents in an exclusive network. Bridge the international gap so agents can generate business amongst your franchise, not outside it.

    Learn More

  • MLSs

    Communicate newly signed listing agreements with members of your MLS in minutes.  Facilitate MLS-wide business collaboration with this custom branded solution to connect members.

    Showcase your expertise of the industry by sharing posts on your profile page. Ask colleagues for their input to spark conversations strictly about real estate.

    Use RESAAS to discover and contact top producing agents in your market. Agents get access to resources to better their business and generate more sales.

    When a transaction completes, refer your partnering client service providers to earn commissionfrom a strategic alliance.

  • More Business

    Get notified instantly when a referral is posted in your area. An increased volume of referral business adds to the revenue generated for your brokerage.

  • Global Internal Network

    Agents communicate in a feed, exclusive to your franchise. View their posts, listings uploaded and real estate conversations, all happening in real-time.

  • Referral Stage Tracking

    A multilingual file storage and sharing solution made available franchise-wide. Streamline communication of franchise direction and strategy.

  • Real-time Listing Sharing

    Members can add their listings in minutes, allowing for transparency amongst the entire MLS and the collection of accurate data.

  • Private Communication

    Activity of members remains private to your MLS. Stay up-to-date with what your members are talking about and the listings they're adding.
